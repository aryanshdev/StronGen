Why Use Strong Passwords ?

Using strong passwords means creating a password that is difficult for others to guess or crack. This helps protect your personal information, such as your email, bank accounts, or social media, from being accessed by people who shouldn't have access to it. Strong passwords can help prevent identity theft, fraud, and other bad things from happening to you.

Creating Strong Passwords

Passwords should have an ideal length of 12 to 15 characters as longer passwords are harder to crack. Also Passwords should contain a combination of capital and small case letters along with numbers and symbols.
Avoid keeping your Birthdate, Your Pet's Name, Your Phone Number, In short your personal information as your password.
